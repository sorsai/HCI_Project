Summer Camp

It’s time for kids to think like real scientists! Using the scientific method, little brains ponder a variety of physics-related topics. From magnetism to density to friction, these future-physicists learn what it means to make hypotheses, perform experiments, record data, and examine results. Through hands-on projects such as designing a pinball run and constructing a mini-catapult, topics such as gravity and motion come alive! And while experimentation takes on many forms – from playing “Sink or Float?” to timing a derby car down a ramp – these students-turned-mini-scientists are continuously challenged as they explore exciting STEM topics.
